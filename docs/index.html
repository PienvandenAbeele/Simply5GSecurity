Simply 5G Security

Our goal is to make the 5G network and especially its security, accessible for everyone who is interested!

Learn more

Welcome

Welcome to simply5gsecurity.com. 5G security is complex and challenging to understand if you are new to the field. We face different security requirements realized by multiple layers and network components. We want to make 5G security understandable and accessible for everyone who needs to understand it or wants to challenge them for fun.

Who is this website for?

The website is for newcomers that want to learn something about 5G security. While we focus on new- comers first, we plan to extend the website to more advanced users.

Who are we?

We are Radix Security a young start-up with the mission to make 5G security accessible. We focus thereby on teaching and tooling. While we have a lot of experience in teaching 5G in seminars or lectures, this website is the first step in the direction of teaching 5G security in an online format.

How do we use acronyms?

5G is full of acronyms. The worst is that the same acronym can have different meanings in different contexts.

How can you support us?

This website lives from feedback and contribution. You will help us if you fill out the feedback form (link to feedback) form and answer the questions. If you have an interesting topic, that you want to talk about in regard to 5G security, please let us know.
